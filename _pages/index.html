Purpose

The Open Source Team works across departments at Allianz to scale open culture and promote best practices of Open Source development. We ensure that Allianz is a good Open Source citizen, from understanding and respecting Open Source licensing to enabling Allianz employees to take active part in the creation of Open Source.


Engage

We invest in and contribute to Open Source projects which are important to us. We encourage Allianz employees to get involved in Open Source.

Exchange

We share our code and processes with the rest of the world as we want technology to benefit as many people as possible.

Promote

We enable and prioritize Open Source code in our engineering teams. We encourage an open culture as we see the practices of the Open Source movement as the best way to collaborate.
